Who Is Jesus Christ?

Do you know Jesus Christ as your personal Savior?

Have you ever received the Lord Jesus into your life? Realize that God loves you and made a way for you through Jesus Christ.

"For God so loved the world, that He gave His only begotten Son, that whosoever believes in Him shall not perish [in hell then the lake of fire], but have everlasting life [in Heaven with Jesus Christ].for God did not send His Son [Jesus Christ] into the world to condemn the world,but that the world through Him might be saved. Whoever believes in Jesus Christ [born-again] is not condemned, but whoever does not believe [is not born-again] is condemned already because they have not believed [been born-again] in the name of the only begotten Son of God. John 3:16-18

God loves you and has a plan for your life

The Bible says that all men are sinners.

"For all [all who have ever lived]have sinned and come short of the Glory of God" Romans 3:23

Our sins have separated us from God. God made man in His image. He gave man the ability to choose right from wrong. We choose to sin. Your sins keep us from God. God's Word also says that sin must be paid for

"For the wages of sin is death." Romans 6:23

Wages mean payment. The payment of our sin is death, hell and the lake of fire after; forever. If we continue in our sin, we shall die without Jesus Christ and be without God forever. The good news is that Jesus Christ paid for our sins

"But God demonstrates His own love towards us, in that while we were still sinners [not born-again] Jesus Christ died for us." Romans 5:8

All of our sins were laid on Jesus Christ on the Cross. He paid our sin debt for us. The Lord Jesus Christ died on the Cross, and He arose from the dead with the keys of death, hell and the grave. Jesus Christ is Alive forevermore. We must personally pray and receive Jesus Christ by Faith
